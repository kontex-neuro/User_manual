### **Overview**

<div class="text-block">
  The EIB Left 32ch is a 32-channel electrode interface board (EIB) specifically designed for use with the X3 series of KONTEX headstages. It features a left-side extension configuration, allowing for streamlined integration in applications with spatial constraints or specific layout requirements.
</div>

### **Key Features**

<div class="text-block1">
    <h4> Channel Capacity </h4>
    Supports 32 recording or stimulation channels.
    <h4> Connector Orientation </h4>
    Left-side extension for optimal alignment with X3 headstages.
    <h4> Compact Layout </h4>
    Designed to minimize footprint and maintain close proximity to neural interfaces.
    <h4> Robust Contacts</h4>
    Ensures secure and stable electrode connection during experiments.
</div>
