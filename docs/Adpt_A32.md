<div class="text-block1">
    <h4> V1.1: Identified by 100012A on PCB </h4>
    <p>A V1.1 of the Adapt-A32 adapter has been introduced with an updated grounding scheme. In this version, previously unused pins are now internally connected to ground (GND) to enhance electrical stability and signal robustness during neural recordings.</P>
    In addition, while the original design was optimized for use with the X3R32 headstage, the updated version is also fully compatible with the X3R64, offering greater flexibility across experimental setups.
</div>
