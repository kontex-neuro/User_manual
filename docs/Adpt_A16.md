### **Overview**

<div class="text-block">
  The Adpt-A-series probe adapter enables seamless integration between acute-style neural probes and the X3 X-HeadstageTM. Designed for versatility, these adapters support 16-, 32-, and 64-channel probes, ensuring broad compatibility with a wide range of neural probes available on the market.
</div>

<div class="text-block1">
    <h4> V1.1: Identified by 100011A on PCB </h4>
    <p>A V1.1 of the Adapt-A16 adapter has been introduced with an updated grounding scheme. In this version, previously unused pins are now internally connected to ground (GND) to enhance electrical stability and signal robustness during neural recordings.</P>
    In addition, while the original design was optimized for use with the X3R32 headstage, the updated version is also fully compatible with the X3R64, offering greater flexibility across experimental setups.
</div>

![test1](Adpt_A16-05.png)

<div class="text-block1">
    <h4> V1 & 1.1 Adapter (SKU: Adpt-A16v1 & v1.1) </h4>
    <Li>Ground (G) and reference (R) signals are independent by default.</Li>
    <br>
    <Li>To short them together at the adapter, solder a short wire between the G and R pads</Li>
